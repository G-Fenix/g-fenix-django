"MyFriends" mobile app by G-Fenix

MyFriends is a social event app that brings people together around shared interests. Whether it's a morning run or a festival, users can easily create plans and invite others. They can also explore nearby events, follow other users, and share their memories with photos or videos. MyFriends offers a whole new way to socialize by blending real-life experiences with technology.

  • 📅 Plan and share activities: from travel and concerts to morning runs and beach volleyball.
  • 🤝 Join nearby plans or invite your friends to your own event.
  • 📍 Discover events based on your location and interests.
  • 💬 Chat with participants, coordinate plans or simply say hello.
  • 👥 Follow other users and build your own friend circle.
  • 📸 Share your event experiences with photos, videos, and reactions.
  • 🎥 Stream event moments directly — just like a social media feed!
  • 🌟 Become a verified host or earn badges through community engagement.
